5 Tips for a Healthy dog diet

1

Choose High-Quality Dog Food

Give safe extras like carrots or cooked meat. Avoid toxic foods like grapes and onions.

2

Add Fresh Whole Foods

Pick food with real meat, no fillers or artificial additives. Match it to your dog’s age, size, and activity level for balanced nutrition.

3

Stick to a Consistent Feeding Schedule

Feed your dog at the same times daily to support healthy digestion and routine.

4

Watch Portions and Monitor Weight

Feed the right amount to prevent obesity. Adjust portions based on activity and body condition.

5

Always Provide Fresh Water

Ensure clean, fresh water is available at all times. Refill and wash the bowl daily.
